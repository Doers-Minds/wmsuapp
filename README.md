# WMSUAPP 
WMSUAPP is designed to help students stay organized, enhance their academic performance, and develop essential learning skills. With a streamlined and user-friendly experience, studying becomes more structured, efficient, and stress-free.

🚀 WMSUAPP – Key Features

🔐 Secure Login & Registration
Students can securely register and activate their accounts using their official Student ID for validation.
Ensures that only verified WMSU students gain access to the platform.

📊 Academic Dashboard
🎓 My Grades
Instant access to academic grades — organized, clear, and available anytime at your fingertips.

📅 My Schedules
View your class schedules in a clean, structured format to stay organized and on time.

📄 Certificate of Registration (COR)
Download and print your official registration documents directly from the app.

📢 WMSU Announcements
Stay updated with the latest university announcements, events, and important notices — all in real time.

📝 Activities Submission
Submit academic activities easily through the app with a flexible and user-friendly interface.

🔔 Smart Notifications
Receive instant alerts for grade updates, announcements, schedule changes, and deadlines.

📚 Study Resources Hub
Access uploaded lecture materials, reviewers, and reference files in one centralized location.

🗂 Academic Progress Tracker
Monitor your academic performance trends and track subject progress throughout the semester.

🏫 Campus Events Calendar
Stay informed about upcoming campus events, seminars, exams, and important academic dates.

💬 Student Support / Help Desk
Submit concerns or inquiries directly to the administration or support team.

🏆 Achievement & Recognition Section
Highlight academic achievements, dean’s list recognition, or campus awards.

🔒 Data Privacy & Security
End-to-end data protection ensuring student information remains confidential and secure.
